Chicken skewers

Description

These chicken skewers seem so simple, but add the cherry sauce and they will be epic.

Ingredients

  • 250 g meat
  • 2 carrots
  • 150 g tomato sauce
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 2 chives
  • 60 ml red wine
  • salt
  • pepper
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Gather everything
  2. Smash everything
  3. Take it to the oven
  4. It's done!
